Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Sunsoar Life Bot is a private household notification service operated by SecuritySphynx LLC. It sends SMS and email reminders about household obligations, schedules, and financial responsibilities to members of a single household.

Information We Collect

This service collects and stores only the information necessary to deliver reminders:

Phone numbers of household members for SMS delivery. Email addresses of household members for email delivery. Task, schedule, and obligation data entered by household members into the shared Notion workspace.

How We Use Information

All collected information is used solely to send scheduled reminders and summaries to household members. We do not sell, share, or disclose any personal information to third parties. Data is not used for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond household reminder notifications.

Third-Party Services

This service uses the following third-party providers to deliver notifications:

Twilio (twilio.com) for SMS message delivery. Gmail (google.com) for email delivery. Notion (notion.so) for data storage.

Each provider processes data according to their own privacy policies. No other third parties receive or access your information.

Data Retention

Reminder logs are retained locally for deduplication purposes and are not shared externally. Notion data is retained until manually deleted by a household member.

Consent and Opt-In

Sunsoar Life Bot is a private, closed service. All message recipients are members of a single household who have provided explicit verbal consent to receive SMS and email notifications. No unsolicited messages are sent. Recipients are added only by the service operator after obtaining direct, verbal confirmation of consent.

By consenting, recipients agree to receive up to two (2) automated SMS messages per day and one (1) weekly summary email containing reminders about household tasks, financial obligations, appointments, work schedules, and school deadlines. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

Any recipient may revoke consent and stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message, or by contacting the service operator at the email address below. Opt-out requests are honored immediately. Opting out does not affect access to the shared Notion workspace.
