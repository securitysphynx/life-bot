Hayden Life Manager is a private household notification service operated by SecuritySphynx LLC.

Service Description

This service sends automated SMS and email reminders to members of a single household regarding chores, financial obligations, work schedules, school schedules, and appointments. Data is managed through a shared Notion workspace.

Eligibility

This service is restricted to members of the Bischoping household who have been explicitly added by the service operator. It is not a public service and does not accept outside users or registrations.

Message Frequency and Charges

Users may receive up to two SMS messages per day and one summary email per week. Message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply. The service itself does not charge recipients.

Opting Out

Any recipient may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message. To opt out of email notifications, contact the service operator. Opting out does not affect access to the shared Notion workspace.

Data and Privacy

Use of this service is subject to our Privacy Policy. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties beyond the delivery providers (Twilio, Gmail, Notion).

Disclaimer

This service is provided as-is for household convenience. Reminders are sent on a best-effort basis and should not be relied upon as the sole means of tracking critical deadlines or obligations. The operator is not liable for missed reminders due to technical failures, carrier issues, or service outages.
